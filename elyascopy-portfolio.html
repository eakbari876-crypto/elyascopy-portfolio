01 — Approach

How I write copy that converts

Every project in this portfolio follows the same principle: copy isn't decoration, it's a conversion tool.

Before writing a single line, I map out the reader's real objection — the hesitation, the fear, the "not right now" — and build the argument that moves them past it.

The result is copy that reads naturally but is engineered, sentence by sentence, to drive a click, a signup, or a sale.

Fitness & coaching apps Consumer tech & e‑commerce Online education FinTech & personal finance
AIDA

Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

The classic four‑step pull, used when the reader isn't actively looking for a solution yet.

PAS

Problem, Agitate, Solution

Leads with the pain point the reader already feels, then resolves it with the offer.

HOOK

Pain‑point‑first hooks

Built for cold traffic that has zero context — earns the next sentence before selling anything.

LIFT

Social proof & scarcity

Numbers, deadlines, and crowd behavior used to convert hesitation into action.
